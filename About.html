Our History

EcoSavvy Living was founded in 2024 by a group of environmental activists who saw the need for practical, accessible sustainability solutions in everyday life. What began as a small community initiative has grown into a thriving organization with multiple programs and an expanding product line.

We started with just three volunteers hosting monthly workshops in a community center. Today, we operate from our own eco-friendly space and reach thousands of people through our online platforms and in-person events.
